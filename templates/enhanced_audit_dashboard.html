Enhanced Audit Process
What the Enhanced Audit Does:
  • Currency Conversion: Converts AUD invoice amounts to USD for rate card comparison
  • Weight Analysis: Uses higher of ship weight or bill weight for chargeable weight calculation
  • Flexible Location Matching: Matches invoices to rate cards using country, city, or port codes

  • Business Rules: Applies minimum charge rules and variance thresholds
Audit Status Meanings:
  • Approved - Variance ≤ 2%
  • Pending - Variance ≤ 10%
  • Review - Variance ≤ 25%
  • Error - Variance > 25% or critical issues
  • Skipped - Ocean freight (no rate card available)
