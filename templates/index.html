Multi-Carrier Logistics Audit System

Next-generation AI-powered freight invoice auditing for Chinese logistics providers with comprehensive multi-modal transportation support

Advanced automation combining EDI processing, PDF extraction, LLM intelligence, and rate card matching across air freight, ocean transportation, domestic delivery, and cross-border logistics.

Key Features

EDI File Processing

Parse X12 EDI format files (210, 214 transaction sets) with automatic data extraction and validation.

Intelligent Auditing

Advanced audit analysis with fuzzy matching, variance calculations, and automated pass/fail determination.

Multi-Modal Support

Support for Air freight, Ocean FCL/LCL, and China Express with specialized pricing models and rate card matching.

Automated Classification

Machine learning powered charge classification with unknown charge detection and manual review workflows.

Advanced Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive shipment reporting, exception analysis, and Excel exports with CW1 shipment grouping and variance analytics.
